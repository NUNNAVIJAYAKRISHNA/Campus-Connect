Campus Sustainability Fair

About this Event

Join us for a day dedicated to sustainability on campus! Explore eco-friendly initiatives, workshops, and exhibits. Learn how to reduce your environmental footprint and contribute to a greener future. Engage with student organizations, local businesses, and experts in the field. Participate in interactive activities, win prizes, and enjoy sustainable food options. This event is a fantastic opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals and make a positive impact on our community and the planet.
